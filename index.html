I am a postdoctoral research associate at Center for Complex Network Research, Northeastern University, where I work with Prof.Albert-László Barabási. My research focuses on understanding success in science and AI for science.

I earned my Ph.D. from Syracuse University and my Master's degree in Information Management from University of Maryland, College Park in 2015. For more details, check my CV.
